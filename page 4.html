AFFILIATE GROUPS

    The Association has the following independent groups affiliated to it:
    St Augustine CSA Choir: to ensure lively liturgy during mass/service
    Pro-Life: Protects, promotes and defend the sanctity and dignity of human life from conception 
    to natural death
    Christian Life Community: To serve the poor and help members to know how to reflect so as 
    to bring Gospel aspects of life.
    Catechists Group: Stepping in place of the priest
    Press Group: To publish information for association
    UoKCSA Prayer Warriors: promotes devotion to a life of prayer, organizes spiritual retreats, 
    novenas and rosary prayers.
    UoKCSA CMA: Looks at the welfare of the male Catholic students
    UoKCSA CWA: Unites and champions for the welfare of the Catholic female students

SHAPE


An enclosed cross with a folded hand, representing the church. The target is to nurture the Christian faith/salvation through prayers.

COLOURING

    Blue- We anticipate for the purity of the heart
    Green- Our heritage; the planet earth that God showed to Adam to conquer and rule. We are 
    called to be responsible over the conservation of the environment.
    Golden yellow- The cross is given a golden yellow color for it was on it that the precious blood 
    of Jesus was shed.
    White- Represents peace. It is a sign of hope. We should be preachers of peace.

SYMBOLS

    Bible- A source of information, a directive. It represents all kinds of reading materials that we 
    use. Let us Be Informed Before Life Ends {BIBLE}, and ask God to give us beneficial 
    knowledge.
    The Cross- A symbol of our salvation. It represents all kinds of challenges that we encounter in 
    our glory. We should pray that God grant us humble hearts, perseverance, patience and sincerity 
    when we face these challenges.
    Let us consider these challenges as bitter pills that we take with gladness to heal us from our 
    iniquities, infirmities and transgressions.
    Folded hands- It signifies the Christian call to prayer life

OUR MOTTO

Our motto is prayer action and sacrifice 

        May the Almighty God grant us full faith in Him, abundance of provisions, enough protection, 
peace of mind, beneficial knowledge and guidance to perform righteous deeds. Noble characters 
and sound mind. AMEN

PREAMBLE 

We, the Catholic Students of University of Kabianga:
 Recognize the supremacy of the Almighty God over all creations;
 Acknowledge His Holy Church;
 Are committed to promote, propagate and sustain the Catholic Christian faith and morals 
among the students, and the entire university;
 Are conscious for our duty before the Almighty God and mankind

UOK CSA OBJECTIVES

1) To create a forum for Catholic Students to meet and share in matters pertaining to the 
faith.
2) To nurture and enhance Christian virtues and principles for religious and family life.
3) To identify, nurture and provide a suitable platform for catholic youth development in 
line with their vocations.
4) To create a link between the members, the chaplaincy and UoK Administration through 
the Dean of students’ Office.
5) To promote apostolic work within and outside the University
6) To take care of the members’ welfare.
7) To organize activities involving the entire community as a way of promoting the catholic 
faith.
8) To set up, equip stock and maintain a Catholic library and bookshop
